We are Business Diversity Business Consultancy

We are Business Diversity Business Consultancy SA, a company formed by professionals with extensive experience gained in over 20 years in providing consultancy services in Human Resources, Health and Safety at Work, Taxation, Accounting, Legal and Administrative Services.

Business Diversity Business Consultancy SA was founded in 2017 as Business Diversity Advisory & Services, EI, was based in Boane, in 2020 the Company underwent a reform changing its name to the current one.
